# ModFi

ModFi is a financial productivity and tracking app built specifically for models. From managing gig payments and tracking expenses to setting savings goals and staying tax-ready, ModFi helps you take control of your money—so you can focus on your craft. Designed with the freelance lifestyle in mind, it's the ultimate tool for models who want to stay organized, empowered, and financially confident.
